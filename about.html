ENTHUSIASTS

Our goal is to bring out your inner craving of learning technology to the next level... Not only theoretical knowledge, but learning by doing. Unleash yourself by joining RAW!

"We believe success comes not from thinking about goals, but taking actions to achieve them."

  • MECHANICAL ASPECT
  • Learn about a variety of mechanical parts used and their used hands-on with our wide variety of resources in RAW team, making yourself comfortable will lead you to know much more about other aspects involved such as links, joints, wrists, etc.
  • CONTROL AND POWER
  • After learning about mechanical aspect, we move to controlling the robot by use of various tools, learning about drives, actuators, controllers and sensors.
  • PROGRAMMING ROBOTS
  • One of the most important aspects about robots where you tell the robot what to do by use of programming languauges, might be tricky at first, but as you progress, they become your second nature and having a good command over basics of robots.
  • EXPLORE
  • After learning with us these aspects, you become capable to be model your own robots and become ready to get your hands dirty and create whatever you dreamt of, the sky's the limit.
